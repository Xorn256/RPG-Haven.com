Review:Legacy of Heroes
By Hibbs

Reviewed by the Boney Dragon

Graphics
Despite what some RPG fans will tell you, graphics ARE important. LoH is proof that time was taken to make the graphics good. This game does not use the tilesets that come with RPG Maker 95, but instead uses the other ASCII set, which makes for some variety. Most of all, every graphical aspect was considered. Towns, and buildings look especially nice as they are decorated with many types of furniture and things. Another thing I liked was the fact that when going through a door in a building, the character went under the top of the door. However, the other parts of the game (combat zones, overworld) need to have more variety and look more realistic. This is a small concern. It is obvious that an effort was made.

Sound
The music is this game is awesome! It really gets you pumped and seemed to be fairly original. Hibbs made good use of the BGM Change command because when something exciting happens the tune goes from quaint villiage music to scary "there's evil about" type music, very slick. The only recommendation I can make is that more sound effects be implemented to add to the atmosphere of the game.

Story
Unlike most RPGM games, this one actually had a story. From the opening sequence (which, by the way, kicks ass), we see that something evil is going on. Then it cuts to the main character and action starts up quickly. The strongest part of the story is the character development. Axel is an interesting one and all the others seem to be there for a reason. Of course, I do have gripes. Things need to be cleared up a bit about why things are the way they are. Some info on the world the characters inhabit and meaningful references to history would be nice. Still, the parts about the evil laughter and the cool opener kept me interested.

Playability
The game flows very smoothly and the battles were well done. However, I encountered two huge bugs that I cannot ignore. When I left the first building, I walked due West and this guy ran into me and the game froze. I restarted, walked slower and everything worked. Well, for a while. When I had to enter the forest, the teleporter didn't work. I walked across and I entered at the other side which was the end of the forest. The whole area did not work! This must be fixed. A larger variety of enemies would be good too. Nice job on text and all but one of the teleporters worked.

Overall Assessment
For an early release, this RPG shows great potential. If some minor problems are fixed, this game will be great. This was not the amateurish effort I expected, continue working!

Grading Scale
A= Excellent game that stands out and is very professionally done. Great job!
B= Very good game that is well worth checking out, but could be improved.
C= There are a few things that are great, but the RPG is pretty average.
D= Mediocre at best. Big problems make it a chore to play.
F= Some have it, some don't. Some can't even pretend they have it. Crap alert!

Legacy of Heroes by Hibbs: C+
-Boney Dragon
