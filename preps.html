Interplay is working on a 3rd person role playing game based on TSR's Dungeons and Dragons world of Planescape. This is an understatement considering that Planescape is based on multiple universes connected by one main multiverse by the name of Sigil. Not a lot of information has been released, but what has looks promising...

Sigil is a large city in which one can find an infinite amount of portals which lead to an infinite amount of universes, assuming you posses the correct key to open the portal. The game will focus on an immortal with amnesia whom you play. You awake on a cold stone slab in the Mortuary of the City of Sigil. You have no idea how you got there, who you are, or any of your past identity(s). You must escape and explore the strange world to uncover the secret of your death and rebirth...
















The interesting mix of graphics and gameplay look like this could be a winner if Interplay can manage to pull through (and avoid a mualing at the hands of Diablo II). Dated to hit shelves summer of '99, Planescape: Torment is a hopeful. Very few pictures have been released, but those I've seen indicate a good combat system of sorts and very nice graphics.

Until more information is released, no assumtion can be made, but Planescape has potential--look for it soon.
