Anachronox by ION Storm is looking to be a really great RPG. Utilizing the Quake II engine (it's nothing like the Quake II engine, but that's what they started out with), Anachronox will feature 189 monsters, 449 NPCs and over 130 places to visit. Some have dubbed this game, the "Chrono Trigger for the Quake era", and that's a pretty good desciption, actually.

A player will be able to use 3 people at a time out of 8 total, and the battle system is rumored to be something of a Final Fantasy clone (but with the QII engine, anything's possible). The graphics of this game look pretty intense, and this is obviously not a game for you Pentium 100 (it REQUIRES at LEAST a Pentium 200 MINIMUM).















There will be no movies during the game, just cutscenes using the same engine, which will cut the size and make for longer game play. Anachronox will span over several planets, and even dimensions.

The game starts out like this:
About two hundred years ago, some unlucky star pilot wandered upon a Sender. A planet-sized reflective sphere sat there in space, long spikes sticking out at regular intervals over its surface. Honing in on a signal one spike seemed to be transmitting, the pilot was probably quite surprised to find himself rocketing toward the Sender, then suddenly in some sort of hyperspace tunnel, then in a different part of space. That is, had his ship been equipped with inertial dampeners, or been shielded for what would be later called Torzewski Radiation. The pulpy, glowing mass that arrived elsewhere in the galaxy was probably quite surprised indeed as what was left of its brain gurgled a bit and fired its last neuron. But soon, ships were using Senders to reach all parts of the galaxy, and after a number of horrible wars, the Senders became the hubs of lucrative trade and galactic civilization. At the center of the universe lay Sender One, and in a time bubble inside this titanic Sender floated the city of Anachronox.

Theory has it that Anachronox was once home to millions of quarantined aliens who slowly died of some sort of horrible plague (it literally means "poison from a previous time"), leaving this city to fall to ruins, to be taken over by the refuse of the galaxy as the center of their nefarious dealings.

Sylvester "Sly" Boots sat in Rowdy's, a seedy bar on the seediest street in a section of Anachronox called the Bricks. After surviving an attack of a group of henchmen, a hairy old man admired Boots' handiwork and asked for his help. Soon Sly was joined by his lackey robot, an old flame turned mercenary, his holographic secretary, and a renegade scientist on a journey across space, across the dimensions, and beyond. Someone is causing the universe to folding in on itself, destroying all existence as we know it! And this group of people are the only ones that believe it is happening, and the only ones that can do something about it....
