Review:Archaic Dreams
By Chaze Watkins

Reviewed by Paladin



Graphics: 7/10
The graphics in this game are pretty original and use different tilesets. But the characters look a little strange and they don't seem to fit into the overall tilesets in the game. Also in the introand the first dungeon there are some parts from FF5, an ice block and a crystal. These things don't fit into the tiles of the page. And I can't judge the combat backgrounds 'cause as in Rising Sun there aren't any enemies. Just an error message. But I like the original idea of the tilesets and it is very good to see that someone is somewhat original.
Sound: 6/10
The sound's aren't so original. The midis aren't ripped off RPG's (some are) but insted from games like Street Fighter and other games. (The airship midi is from Street Fighter, Ryu's song). Well there is a variation, and music changes when you enter a village or a church. So that is a sure plus. But since some music just doesn't fit in so good, I gave the game a 6.

Story: 6/10
The story in this game is pretty good. And the intro is very original. But right away you don't understand much of the story. (But of course the demo is very short so I can't really count this as a bad thing, but anyway) It is about some crystals that control genes or something like that. Well I at least didn't get much of the story. The game begins with a boy who is a computer freak and somehow gets transformed into a hero while he is asleep. Well as I said hard to understand.

Playability: 8/10
Well this game should have gotten a straight 10 but there is a big bug. The enemy encounters don't work. That is a pretty big bug so I took away 2 points for that. Nothing more to say though. all the teleporters work perfectly and there are no other bugs except for the enemies one.

Overall Assessment
Well in overall this game is actually not totally amateur made. It has some spine in it and some hilarious comments. But some bugs and downlets make this game not a sure choice when downloading. But as I said with Rising Sun. If you are making a game, download this one to get ideas. Downloading it for your own pleasure is a bad idea since the demo is about 5 minutes long. But if you want to test it then go ahead.
Grading Scale
A= Excellent game that stands out and is very professionally done. Great job!
B= Very good game that is well worth checking out, but could be improved.
C= There are a few things that are great, but the RPG is pretty average.
D= Mediocre at best. Big problems make it a chore to play.
F= Some have it, some don't. Some can't even pretend they have it. Crap alert!

Archaic Dreams by Chaze Watkins: C

-Paladin
