Here you will find, every good translation patch for the NES and SNES, and others, with the version number and or percentile of completion, the platform that its for and a link back to the translator's home page. If there is no link back to the translator's homepage, its because they currently don't have one. You will need an IPS patcher to patch the ROMs and make them English. There are three different ones to choose from on the Utilities page. Click here to go there.
