Welcome to the RPGMaker reviews section! In this section we take RPG's that people upload, review them, and give them out for downloading and scoring by our viewers. We have tried to make review reading and scoring as easy as possible. Just click on the name of the RPG to get the full review done by the staff, and to score an RPG just fill in the form under "Score the RPG" and "Submit" it (Note: The viewers scoring is on a number basis, please do not send B+ or C- grades, just send numbers such as 8.9 or 5.0) You may also E-Mail to author to tell them you're thoughts.
