Review:Ragnurak
By Greame

Reviewed by Paladin

Graphics: 4/10
What graphics? This game is really poorly made in the ways of graphic. The tilesets are combined wrong and this guy doesn't know how to use the event function. Too much blue stuff. The decoration is really bad and there are tons of graphical glitches. There are very little areas to play and they are very poorly and hasty made. Nothing else can be said.
Sound: 0/10
Once again, what sound? There is no sound or music whatsoever, but the events say that there should be music apparently the author didn't send it with the game. So no points to score here. I really can't judge this because I have no music files to hear.
Story: 3/10
And again, what story. the game begins with an (angel?) and you go through a room and then meet a bad guy. Who want's to take over the universe with a cup? (Why not a bottle?) Then after you've killed him you become human and get into a hut. The game continues in the same spirit of letting the tester be happily unaware of the story. This story (I must use this word) SUCKS!
Playability: 6/10
No real bugs except for the fact that the game crashes after you have defeated the second boss. I don't know how to fix this so, I have to cheat myself to get through the rest of the demo. The teleporters are working and so are the events but the game isn't very well made any ways.
Overall Assessment
The maker of this game really didn't seem to try very hard with the making of this game, and they should probably not try to improve upon it either (unless they they can do miracles) my best idea for it would be to just scrap the game completely, and try another one (perhaps trying this time).
Grading Scale
A= Excellent game that stands out and is very professionally done. Great job!
B= Very good game that is well worth checking out, but could be improved.
C= There are a few things that are great, but the RPG is pretty average.
D= Mediocre at best. Big problems make it a chore to play.
F= Some have it, some don't. Some can't even pretend they have it. Crap alert!

Ragnurak by Greame: D-
-Paladin
