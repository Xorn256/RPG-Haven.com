Revenant is the new Dark Fantasy RPG and Adventure by Edios Interactive it features a new visually stunning and changing environment. You can interact with over 40 characters as you choose from a wide variety of weapons, spells, armor and magical items and battle hand to hand in a 3D fighting tradition. Revenant combines the realism and excitement of an adventure with the exploration, character development, and gaming longevity of an RPG, Making it possibly one of the best Action RPG games this year.











The main focus of Revenant is the story, but if that was all the game was, then Edios would probably be publishing a book and not an RPG. Combat is where the story comes to a head. The combat system in Revenant is different than what we are used to in traditional RPGs, it is real-time, not turn based for one thing. It is real-time action battling with many different techniques and skills to chose from.

As you explore the world, the character will be in his normal walk mode, but once an enemy comes within range, he will automatically draw his sword and get into his ready position. From here, you will have more control over your character than you ever had in an RPG before. You can choose from multiple strikes, movements, leaps, and parries as you fight your opponent. Some screen shots to the left will give you a better idea of the walking/fighting engine.

It's not entirely up to your hand-eye skill either. Depending on your character's skill in certain weapons and areas, you will gain new strikes and combos that you can unleash on your unsuspecting opponent. Even if your opponent is blocking, you may still get through and do some damage, providing your skill is high enough.

The spell system is also interesting; it revolves around many different types of magic (i.e. Earth Magic, Fire Magic and Life Magic) using these different types of magic you can create all new spells and enchantments.

In all I believe that Revenant will be a great addition to the Action RPG industry. In fact I believe that if it comes out in time, it could cause problems for Big upcoming RPG games such as Diablo2.



FEATURES
Real-time 3D polygonal characters with motion captured animations provide unparalleled realistic combat. SVGA graphics with 65,000 colors. Day and night cycles plus special animated lighting effects. Developed using Direct 3D - compatible with nearly every 3D accelerator on the market. Spectacular environments where gamers are free to explore following the non-linear storyline, hacking a few monsters along the way. Fantasy artwork developed with the help of Canadian artist, Den Beauvais, a premier fantasy and science fiction illustrator. All original music created exclusively for Revenant by Ronie Moorings of the European band, Xymox. Full 3D surround sound with environmental audio effects. Multiplayer for up to 4 players on modem, lan and Mplayer.com.
