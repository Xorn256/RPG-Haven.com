Lunar Complete,

Lunar complete is Working Designs' completely redone, renewed and restored version of the entire "Lunar: The Silver Star Story", Working designs has taken the entire story (originally in the old 16 bit, Sega-CD format) and has completely redone it in the 32 bit Playstation format adding more superior graphics and effects.

The entire story is actually a couple CDs long, (Even on the Playstation, it wouldn't go on one CD) And all the CDs for it are available in a nifty Lunar: Silver Star Package for about $60-$70 and game is due to be released for the good old Playstation sometime 1st quarter '99.

To those of us like me, who have played the game and are starting to get the urge to play it again. this could be the perfect time to do such a thing. The new version looks a great deal more impressive than the original and due to this the experience of playing it could only be better.









I for one am rather excited that this great game like Lunar: (the only successful RPG on Sega-CD... Or actually the only successful "Game" on Sega-CD) will finally be open to Those who never got a chance or those who didn't make the terrible mistake of buying a Sega-CD (Me unfortunately). They can now Appreciate Working Designs' masterpiece in a better way and on a better system.
