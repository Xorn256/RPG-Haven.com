## RPG-Haven.com (1998–2002)

RPG-Haven.com was an independent RPG community website active during the late 1990s and early 2000s.

The site functioned as a community hub focused on RPG Maker games, indie RPG projects, reviews, previews, and user-submitted or hosted community content during the pre–modern social media era of internet gaming culture.

---

### Historical Context

RPG-Haven operated in a period where RPG communities were primarily distributed through:
- independent fan websites
- forum-driven communities
- download and hosting hubs
- curated “portal-style” RPG sites

This was prior to the dominance of centralized video platforms and modern game distribution ecosystems.

---

This repository preserves the original RPG-Haven.com website files in their existing structure, largely unmodified, for historical and archival purposes.
