Reviewed game: Happy Valley
Made by: Billl & ThaYne
Made In: RPG Maker95

Reviewed by: Paladin



Graphics: 80%
This game has something really original! They have pictures of people that are talking. This is the same as in any new RPG out for sale and it is the first game that uses this function (I will not draw away any point for the not so good quality of the faces 'cause this is really original and I like original stuff). The tilesets are the most common ones (except for the original) but Bill and ThaYne have mixed the tilesets after their own needs. It turned out to be really good. This game is somewhat glitch free, but you can still find 'em. They are probably things that the authors didn't see 'cause they aren't very big. Pretty good graphics that is! And they even have pictures of the items you get! That is very original. But the characters didn't quite melt in to the rest of the environment in the game. They were somewhat colorless. But still good.
Sound: 50%
There is a healthy use of sounds in this game, and they are everywhere. When you open a door (the sound for opening doors shows that people in Happy Valley don't care about the welfare of their doors, actually I got tired of the door sound :) and every chest sounds. So there are plenty of sounds but not a single midi file. This is pretty much a downlet. Any game gets a little dull when there are no midis. But that is all.

Story: 80%
I like the story in this game. The idea of the school and the four magic elements. Original at least that cure magic isn't white but green. And I really think that something was high while Bill and ThaYne got the story for the Happy Valley. Misuse os "herbs"? Who knows. Download it and you'll see. ^_^ But the characters could have been a little more interesting. I lost contact with the characters about halfway (of course the talking pics of them help but...) A little more work with the characters is needed. There is also a explanation for everything you do unlike other games that just make you go somewhere. Good plot (if maybe a little weird) and it makes you like the game's funny interface.

Playability: 80%
My god! What is this! Everyone forget the enemies in their game nowadays! Make game disks! Much easier! Well as you might have guessed there are no enemies in this game so no judgment here but the rest of the game is bug free and no crashes that I noticed. But get a hold of yourselves all of you making games! Pack the enemies too! Oh my...

Overall grade and personal comments
This games sure shows a lot of potential! Just a little adjustments and it has the capability of being great! Something worth downloading, check it out all of you.


Grade of this game is: 78%
