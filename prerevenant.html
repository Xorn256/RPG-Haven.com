Revenant,

Revenant is the new Dark Fantasy RPG Action RPG by Edios Interactive, it features a new and very impressive overhead diablo style view and an always changing environment. You can interact with over 40 characters online as you choose from a wide variety of weapons, spells, armor and magical items and battle hand to hand in a not so Diablo tradition. Revenant combines the realism and story depth of an adventure with the exploration, character development, and gaming longevity of an RPG, Making it possibly one of the best Action RPG games this year.










Edios' main focus for Revenant was the story, but if that was all the game was, then Edios would probably be publishing a book and not an RPG. Combat is where the story comes to a head.

The combat system in Revenant is different than what we are used to in traditional RPGs, it is real-time, not turn based (kinda like Diablo) but there is one huge difference in comparison to Diablo. In Diablo you would attack, or use magic, That's it! not much else as far as combat went. But in this there are many, many different combat moves and techniques you can use to vanquish your opponent. Instead of the standard "Hack" you can go all out with a jumping sword slash, followed by a shield parry, followed by a slash to the gut and ended with your sword through there head. As you can imagine it could get very intricate.

It's not entirely up to your hand-eye skill either. Depending on your character's skill in certain weapons and areas, you will gain new strikes and combos that you can unleash on your unsuspecting opponent. Even if your opponent is blocking, you may still get through and do some damage, providing your skill is high enough.

The spell system is also interesting; it revolves around many different types of magic (i.e. Earth Magic, Fire Magic and Life Magic) these realms of magic besides doing spells of there type (Fire doing Fire based spells, ect) you can combine these different types of magic to create all new spells and enchantments.

In all I believe that Revenant will be a great addition to the Action RPG industry. In fact I believe that if it comes out in time, it could cause problems for Big upcoming RPG games such as Diablo2.
