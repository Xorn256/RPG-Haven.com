Made by: RM
Made In: RPG Maker95

Format: Game Disk

Reviewed by: Paladin



Graphics: 50%
No it is not the tilesets I'm complaining on, but it is the many glitches that show up here and there within the game. The town looks pretty bad, nothing except for a green dragon that is custom made. Nothing more to say though... but it can be improved. I'm waiting for the next demo version.
Sound: 80%
The in-game-music is good and some parts are rip-offs but some are actually pretty original. I like it, but this game lacks something (can't anyone make a perfect game with both monsters, SFX and music?) no sound effects. So I'm sorry not a full score there.

Story: 60%
Well little info about the characters and a not so good intro make this game kinda boring at the end (not that the demo was long). Improvement here are actually needed. More action in the dialogues and in the events. something more to the plotline and not so straightforward.

Playability: 70%
There is a big bug in the game. Actually there are several. Now when you encounter an enemy you have to press the talk/search button to actually fight it. And after a few enemies the game music stops. No music at all. This I do not know how to fix. And after the music has stopped you have to wait 3 seconds(Who knows).Also a bug with the money you get after a battle. Three very big bugs (but as you see this guy has enemies!).

Overall grade and personal comments
The game is repairable but for now maybe not so good. It seems that the game ends after you defeat the evil dragon king. This would then be the shortest RPG in history. Big plus though, this guy made a game disk!


Grade of this game is: 65%
