SNESSOR v2.1

With this program you can take the sound effects out of any ROM and extract it to your computer. Great if you want to listen to sound effects in games, and if you want to create your own game, but need sounds.
Download it here.

Features:

  • Can search .SMCs(or any other file)
    for SNES-type sounds. 
  • Can play each on command, at the selected speed.
  • Can save each separately, or all at once.
  • Can write to WAV and SND files.
  • Can replace any one sample with
    your own WAV file.

Supports:

  • Super NES


Platform:

  • Dos

    SPC Play v0.200

    For playing the actual music in a game.

SPC Play is a program that opens a ZSNES saved state, and plays the music that was playing at that moment. Just save a state at a great music part, and then you can listen to it later, with this.
Download it here.

Features:

  • Easy to use
  • Very helpful for capturing music
