Guardian's Crusade,

Guardian's Crusade is an upcoming game by Activision, the game seems to very much copy final fantasy 7, as far as the fighting engine goes. As for the other aspects of the games, (besides the cheesy characters and cartoon aspects) Activision seems to have made one of there first original RPGs. Though the game was almost destroyed before it even came out, due to impopularity and Activision being unable to get much publicity for it in the RPG world, until Tamsofts' help which should give Activision the edge it needs to get out from under some of the big RPG makers we've all come to know as Square Soft. and get the game out in stores.









The game is not role-playing as it is normally know, it seems to follow a strange and new style of Activision's own. Instead of following the experience and leveling system, the game follows a teacher/student relationship, between the young knight and his sidekick. which as a result of the players interaction can change into several different fully grown monsters to help you on your way.

Unfortunately if you are a person who likes traditional RPG this game may not suet your liking, What with the funny and goofy aspects and cartoon looking characters, generally the game looks for a seven year old to play it. But on the other hand if you like lighter, non gothic or more down-to-earth games this could be the best RPG ever for you.

Guardian's Crusade starts out as a young knight (you) on a quest to unravel the mysterious past of his companion, a baby monster. (which looks a lot like a baby pink marshmallow with two eyes.)

Instead of following the experience and leveling system ,the game follows a teacher/student relationship, between the young knight and his sidekick. which as a result of the players interaction can change into several different fully grown monsters to help you on your way. In addition to this the game promises a large cast of what Activision calls "Living toys"- additional sidekick which you can summon into battle to help you by healing, defending, fighting, ect.

Activision also promises many intricate puzzles for the player to solve along with many sub-quest that can be won. In all the game's strange a pastel like exterior gives way to an "interesting" and innovative gameplay. The game's release date is estimated to be around March '99.
