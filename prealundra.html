Alundra was produced by Working Designes, makers of such games as Vay, and Lunar. Alundra although previously in japan the well known RPG is soon to make its way to US costs somtime around 1st quarter '98.

Alundra is very much diffrent from its predisecor though, this time it takes more of a Zelda point of view and a story that is also very much Zelda.







Alundra follows the exploits of a young explorer haunted by an intense dream. He is driven by a man known as the Guardian to locate an evil entity sworn to destroy the world. Alundra heads for the open sea to begin his journey.

Unlike the original adventure, Alundra has an uncanny Zelda-style interface, both in its action and in its storytelling. And very much like Zelda is the battle sequences, which take place in real-time.

Working Designs has been working on the English translation of the game for quite a while, and it is hoped that the game should reach completion by the end of December.
