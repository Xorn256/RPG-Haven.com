DarkStone,

DarkStone by Delphine Software is said to be the Diablo look alike for this year, yet also at the same time it is nothing like Diablo. While its true that most of the interface and battle engine look like Diablo as well as some of the sound and music, it also gives completely new and different aspects to that of that Diablo.

Diablo was said to be an action game with some rpg accepts. While Darkstone is more like an RPG with some action aspects.

















Darkstone features many, many quests and sub quest also many unique weapon that must be given to certain ncps to go on with your quests. Also the spell system is more like the traditional RPGs, unlike the weapon-like projectiles that Diablo made them, also healing and defensive spells can only affect the person casting them.

Much of the games is still unsaid but so far there have been a little bit of problems with the AI. Problems such as other part of your party having path finding problems making them a little glitch on the following side of things, also they seem to walk in front of you and get in the way when trying to pass through doors and such. But these problems are said to be fixed very soon.

Still, even though it seems to be so very much like Diablo DarkStone has a few tricks up its sleeve. Unlike Diablo DarkStone uses polygonal characters instead of sprites which defiantly gives it an edge over last year's greatest. Because of the fact they use polygons in Darkstone you can get any among many different views and still keeping perfect picture quality. Everything from a birds eye view to a "Whats that on that guy's face" view can be accomplished in the 360 degree fully turnable/zoomable view. This among many other aspects should help DarkStone with getting away from that, "Hey, its a Diablo clone" that most poeple whould say the second they were to see the game.

With great depth of color and a great realistic polygonal engine, DarkStone could be the best RPG to play until the very much awaited "Goliath PC RPG" Diablo2 to be released.

In all, even though it copies many aspects of Diablo that can't be ignored, DarkStone still has enough uniqueness to make it a great and addicting dungeon crawler. And will be releaced somtime April '99, for PC and Playstation.
