The OHRRPGCE (Our Hamster Republic Role Playing Game Contruction Engine) is an easy-to-use RPG editor which ranks as one of the best out there. The customability and user friendly atmosphere makes it really easy to make a nice game in a flash. The editor which we feature is only a demo to the great editor which the programmer is still working on. Also, the creators own game (Bob the wandering Hamster) is also available for direct download to give you examples and to play for yourself...
Very soon, I (Chaos) will be available for questions and help for the use of this engine, and you can e-mail me with questions or comments. As with all other featured editors...we will post and rank any and all games sent to us (please--no vulgarities).
